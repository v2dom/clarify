Terms of Use

Clarify is an open-source personal project designed to help users visualize their Spotify listening habits. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is. This page outlines the policies regarding the use of this service and data handling practices.

Information Collection and Use

Clarify does not store any personal data on external servers nor share your information with third parties. Clarify temporarily accesses your Spotify data to generate statistics and visualizations within your session. For convenience, some settings may be saved locally in your browser’s Local Storage. This data never leaves your device.

Manage app access on Spotify

Revoking Access and Deletion of Data

Clarify does not retain any user data. However, if you'd like to revoke Spotify permissions, you can do so from your Spotify account settings. To clear locally saved data, simply clear your browser's cache and site data.

Your Agreement

By using Clarify, you agree to allow temporary access to your Spotify data solely for the purpose of generating insights. No data is stored permanently or used outside the scope of this application.
